Hello, my name is Amornthep Jeramanapong. This website has a purpose to share everything about me. I aspire to become a software engineer, which led to the creation of this website. Please take this into consideration. I was born in a modest family in Surat Thani Province, Thailand. When I was a child, my parents bought me a computer for me to use. Since around 2010, when computers started to become more popular and people were getting excited about them, I became someone who loved playing games a lot. As I grew up, I started thinking about my future career. I had an idea that I wanted to work with computers. In 2019, I got introduced to coding, which sparked a great interest in me. In 2021, I entered the last years of high school, focusing on science, mathematics, and technology. During that time, the COVID-19 pandemic hit, which kept me at home and allowed me to experiment with many things. This piqued my interest even more. In my fifth year of high school, I engaged in various activities, practiced different languages, and firmly decided to become a software engineer. Thank you.
